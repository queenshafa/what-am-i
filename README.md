# What Am I?

What am I? What are you? What are we? Well.. you lucky i created a website to check what are you :p

What am I is a simple website created by TailwindCSS and Vanilla JS that shows what age group you're in. (And it's based on my research okay, at least that's what google said about group of generations..)

Oh! And, submitted for [Flavortown!](https://flavortown.hackclub.com/)

# So like.. how to use it?

It's as easy as flipping your hand, silly. I have provided an input box to input your year. Put your birth year and once you're sure it's correct (lol who got it incorrect anyway) click the 'Check!' button and boom, the card will show your age group.
